A learning journal

Compounding

I'm Liam, a rising junior in Charleston, South Carolina. I'm learning how money actually works by doing it: shadowing a financial advisor, building real financial plans, and trading my own positions in the market. I'm also actively meeting with professionals who work across different parts of the financial world, both to get real exposure to the field and to understand how its different pieces fit together.

This is not a list of budgeting tips. It is a running record of what I get right, what I get wrong, and the conversations that change how I think. I write things down here while they are still fresh.
